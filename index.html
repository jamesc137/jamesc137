BitNations is a minimalist nation simulation game that was never officially launched but gained surprising popularity organically within a year of its existence. Initially discovered by users, the game quickly found its way into schools in Sweden, where it was utilized as a learning tool for Model UN. This grassroots interest led to its spread through a network of educational institutions in Sweden and across the U.K. and Europe.

As students began to engage with the game outside of class, its popularity continued to grow. BitNations eventually expanded beyond Europe, reaching Asia, Australia, California, and now, the globe.
